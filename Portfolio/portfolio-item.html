Text Based Adventure Game

Python

Waking up to an apocalypse. You have just woken up, like most days, you follow the same routine, got out of bed, brushed your teeth, had a shower, got dressed. As you are making your breakfast, you check your smartphone to check if you have any notifications to realise your phone has no connection, you try turn it off move around to try find a signal with no luck. You step outside to see if your phone can pick up a signal there. You open your door to emptiness. You cannot see a single person in any direction you look. This is when you notice a strong sense of danger, every fibre in your body is telling you that there is something terribly wrong.

What are the decisions? 1.Go outside, yes (Look for survivors, food, and supplies, 2.- Go to the loft. 2a. Look for supplies. 2b. Find a map that has directions to a shelter not too far away. 3. Go to the neighbor's house, see if they know anything. ) Left - None of the neighbor's answer the door, you go back home. - Trust other survivors Yes (Survive and thrive as a community.) 4. -No (Take your chance out there on your own, as you were not feeling safe with the other saviours. You end up finding a research facility that may be able to help you find answers about what started this whole apocalypse. ) Right - When none of the neighbor's answer the doors, you do not go back home, you try find anyone by walking around aimlessly, you then get attacked and die. What are potential endings? 5.Survive and thrive during the apocalypse, by working with other survivors. 6.Die in the first week, as you run out of supplies, and you get attacked trying to find more supplies. 7.Use research facility to find out what has happened.
